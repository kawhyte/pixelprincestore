Verified against the real dev server: a live /api/claim-art request produced a correct ZIP whose PNG SHA-256-matched the Cloudinary master, and the Sanity downloads counter incremented correctly.

Email-Gated Downloads

PLAN-03-critical-email-capture-engine.md
CRITICAL

The heart of the strategy: free art in exchange for an email. Visitor picks art → enters email → gets a 72-hour download link by email → lands on your list. Welcome email included.

📬Do domain verification (SPF/DKIM) the same day — without it your emails land in spam and the whole engine looks broken.
🏆Milestone: after this plan, every download grows your list — the #1 job of the site.
